Internal Research Note: Tesla

Previous recommendation: Hold with selective accumulation only for high-risk portfolios.
Rationale: Tesla has strong brand strength, software optionality, and energy storage growth, but valuation sensitivity remains high.
Risks: margin pressure, EV demand cyclicality, price cuts, regulatory investigations, and execution risk in autonomous driving.
Analyst instruction: Before recommending a buy, compare current valuation with delivery growth, free cash flow, and peer EV multiples.
